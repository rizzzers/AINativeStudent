Questions

What parents ask us

Does my student need any technical background? +

Not at all. Our programs start from zero and are designed for students of any background. We teach AI as a professional and creative skill, not as computer science.

How much time does it require per week? +

Courses require 2–3 hours/week. Group cohorts add a 1-hour live session. 1:1 coaching is typically 1 hour/week with optional additional work. Everything is designed to fit around school.

How is this different from just having them use ChatGPT? +

Using AI casually is very different from understanding it strategically. We teach students how to apply AI tools professionally in their specific career direction, with context, judgment, and skill that casual use never develops.

Is this for high school students, college students, or both? +

Both, and that's intentional. High school students use the program to differentiate in college applications, develop an edge in AP and honors coursework, and arrive at college already ahead of their peers. College students focus on internship readiness, career-specific AI applications, and standing out in a competitive job market. The 1:1 coaching is what makes each experience specific to your student's stage.

Apply and we'll schedule a free 20-minute parent call. We'll ask about your student's goals, learning style, and schedule and make a specific recommendation. No pressure.

What's your satisfaction guarantee? +
